About Our Boundaries

The Southwood Civic Club neighborhood is a defined residential area in Houston, Texas. The community is bordered by four main streets that form its outer limits.

  • North: Rosedale Street
  • South: Oakdale Street
  • West: Sauer Street
  • East: Tierwester Street

Interior streets within the neighborhood include Wichita Street, Palm Street, and Southmoore Blvd. The community sits just west of Cullen Boulevard and north of N MacGregor Way along Brays Bayou. Southwood is centrally located and close to the University of Houston, the Texas Medical Center, and Texas Southern University. Other nearby attractions include the Houston Zoo, Hermann Park, and the Museum District.
