About the Civic Club

Who we are and what we do

The Southwood Homeowners Association (Civic Club) exists to protect neighborhood standards, support property values, and encourage positive relationships among neighbors. We coordinate communications, host meetings, support neighborhood safety efforts, and provide resources related to deed restrictions and community projects.

Our board works with residents to keep Southwood welcoming, informed, and well-maintained. We encourage homeowners to attend meetings, share concerns, and volunteer in neighborhood programs.
